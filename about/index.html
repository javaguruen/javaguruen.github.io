I'm a 48 years old man living in Bergen, Norway. Married and father of two teenage boys. Currently a Senior developer at Ambita but these pages and the blog are my private opinions and workings.

I'm primarily a backend developer with special interests in functional programming, architecture and APIs. In my daily work I code in Kotlin and Java but have many years of experience with Groovy and Scala and some knowledge of JavaScript and Python.

In my spare time I like to read popular science, keep up with trends in software development, experiment with new languages and framework and play football (soccer). I love to share my experience in writing and do frequent presentations both internally at work but also publicly in user groups and communities around Norway. I've also been a speaker at conferences lik Boosterconf and JavaZone.

For what is left of my spare time I'm one of the volunteers running javaBin Bergen (java user group in Bergen) and have on several occasions held workshops on coding for kids.
